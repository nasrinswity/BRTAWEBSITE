    About Us

    Bangladesh Road Transport Authority is a regulatory body for ensuring order in the road transport sector and road safety in Bangladesh.

    1:Controlling and regulating road transport by executing motor vehicle acts, issuing route permits, and fixing rates and fares of buses and trucks
    2:Conducting regular activities like: Issuing driving license, fitness certificates, registration certificates and Driving Instructor's licenses
    3:Registering schools for motoring
    4:Organizing and conducting workshop seminars for delivering information regarding safe driving and traffic regulations
    5:Making research and development for developing ideas and methodologies for safe road transport and traffic system

    Driver's License Instructions

    • Visit the BRTA Service Portal to create an account.
    • 1:Complete the online application form and upload the necessary documents.
    • 2:Schedule your driving test and pass the theoretical and practical exams.
    • 3:Pay the applicable fee and collect your driver's license.
